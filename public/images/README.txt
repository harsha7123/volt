Put your real photos here, then reference them in src/data/site.js.

Examples:
  hero / product photos:  '/images/gridx-ultrafast.jpg'
  installation photos:    '/images/install-manikonda.jpg'
  team photos:            '/images/ujwal.jpg'

Any field left as '' shows a branded placeholder, so the layout
always looks complete even before you add photos.

Tip: use your own installation photos and product images you have
rights to (e.g. from your hardware supplier). Avoid using other
companies' branded marketing renders.
